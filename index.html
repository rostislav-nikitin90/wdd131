Montenegro

Montenegro is a small country in Southeastern Europe on the Adriatic coast, known for its picturesque landscapes, including mountains, lakes and beaches. It attracts tourist with its mild subtropical climate and rich cultural heritage, represented by ancient cities and Mediterranean cuisine. The friendliness of the locals and safety make Montenegro an ideal place for recreation and travel.
