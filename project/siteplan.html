Site Name: Montexplore - Montenegro Travel Guide

Domain Name: montexplore.com

This name represents a company that specializes in providing a Montenegro travel guide. The name Montexplore is a combination of two words: Montenegro and explore. It is catchy and easy to remember, and clearly conveys the idea of exploring Montenegro. It also has a modern and adventurous feel, which is perfect for a travel guide website.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide up-to-date detailed information on local current events and tourist attractions to visit and explore in Montenegro, including a photo gallery of local tourist coastal towns and national parks, to represent a Montenegro travel guide.

Scenarios:

What are the best places to visit in Montenegro on the Adriatic coast?

Where can I find more information about local events and tourist attractions?

The primary color will be used as a light background on which dark text and images will be displayed, and as light text on a darker background.

The secondary color will be applied to display dark text on a light background.

Accent 1, Accent 2, Accent 3 and Accent 4 will be used for headings as a darker background on which light heading text will be displayed, and can also be used to display links and button colors.
